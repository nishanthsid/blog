# Nishanth's Blog

> *Understanding computers from the ground up.*

This is my personal collection of documentation, articles, and experiments in systems programming.

Rather than treating operating systems and low-level software as black boxes, I like understanding how they work internally—one abstraction at a time.

Most of what you'll find here revolves around asking simple questions and gradually building up to the implementation.

## My Thoughts

I believe that the best way to understand computers is to build them piece by piece.

Instead of memorizing APIs, I prefer understanding the abstractions they hide.

Instead of treating systems software like a black box, I try to understand them by asking questions like:

- Why do they exist?
- What problem do they solve?
- How would I build it myself?
- I tend to do a DFS into the topics by asking questions repeatedly

If that sounds interesting, you'll probably enjoy reading this blog.
